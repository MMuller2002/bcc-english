Editing session | 0-35 minOne Targeted Improvement
0-35 minv

This is not the time for major structural changes. Identify your highest-value improvement and do that one thing well.

If your speech is working:Don't rewrite it. Mark it up for delivery instead. Add notation marks to every key moment.
If your conclusion is weak:That is the highest-value edit. Rewrite your call to action first. Everything else can wait.
If you are under 3 minutes:Add content to your thinnest argument. One more piece of evidence and one more explanation sentence.
If you are over 5:30:Identify the most repetitive section. Cut the weakest example, not your strongest argument.
