Key concepts | 10-25 minThe PAP Framework
10-25 minv

Copy this vocabulary into your glossary.

TermDefinitionExample
PersuasionAny attempt to influence thinking, feeling or behaviour through communication.An ad, a speech, a petition.
ContentionThe main argument the author wants you to accept."Eating less meat saves the planet."
AudienceThe intended group the text is created for.Teenagers, parents, voters.
PurposeThe reason the text was created -- to sell, inform, entertain, advocate.To sell a product.
PositionWhere the author stands -- their point of view on the topic."Fast food is harmful to teenagers."

Quick check -- PAP framework

A charity ad says: "Every 60 seconds a child goes hungry. You can change that." What is the PURPOSE of this text?
